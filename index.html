Have you ever wanted to play a FPS in a Sci-Fi World, where your conscious is inside the game? Well now you can! The long awaited "Gun Gale Online", a game created by "Toby Weeb Miyazaki", has been released in late 2020. The game is released on a full dive Virtual Reality, which transfers you memory and consciousness into a machine.
